My Story

Welcome to my personal website, where you can learn a little bit about Amelia Rider.

Early Life

I grew up in the small town of Big Sur, California. The population is very small, and it's a tight knit community. Growing up there, I learned a deep respect and appreciation for the natural world.

  • Learn More

Elementary School

I attended the same small, Episcopal school from Pre-K through 8th grade. My class had 26 students. The school was very supportive, and I was able to explore interests in math, science, and the arts.

  • Learn More

Current Life

I now live in the Inner Richmond district in San Francisco with my boyfriend. The neighborhood is very friendly and homey. I work at the company Omada Health as a Solutions Engineer, and I'm currently attending the Actualize Coding Bootcamp to become a software engineer. In my free time, I like training for running races, and I completed my 3rd marathon in April 2025.
